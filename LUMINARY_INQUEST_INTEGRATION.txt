WHAT IS THE LUMINARY INQUEST?
===============================================================================

OFFICIAL DESIGNATION:
- Name: The Luminary Inquest
- Authority: Direct reporting to highest imperial authority
- Mandate: Divine mandate from Helios, the Sun God
- Purpose: Regulation of existence itself (officially "protection of civilization")

COMMON NAMES:
- The Inquest
- Luminaries
- Sun Enforcers
- (Unofficial names "spoken quietly and rarely written")

JURISDICTION:
- Operates freely across all imperial territory
- Supersedes local law, city guards, and regional governance
- "When the Inquest arrives, jurisdiction collapses inward"
- Authority based on documentation, magical verification, and doctrinal interpretation

===============================================================================
METHODS AND OPERATIONS
===============================================================================

ENFORCEMENT APPROACH:
✓ Investigations: Sudden and without warning
✓ Accusations: Made publicly
✓ Enforcement: Immediate
✓ Process: Suspects isolated, restrained, judged on the spot
✓ Verification: Documentation review, magical checks, doctrinal authority
✓ Executions: Conducted openly and efficiently
✓ Philosophy: "Visibility reinforces order"
✓ Collateral: Recorded as acceptable loss in service of security

REPUTATION:
- Widely known for brutality
- Feared throughout the empire
- Deep resentment and quiet hatred from citizens
- Disrupts daily life, commerce, and community trust
- Taverns fall silent when Inquest insignia appear
- Markets empty, neighbors avoid eye contact

DANGER OF INNOCENCE:
⚠️ Innocence does not guarantee safety
⚠️ Missing paper, mismatched record, clerical inconsistency = fatal suspicion
⚠️ Appeals are rare, reversals almost nonexistent

===============================================================================
ENFORCEMENT OF EXISTENCE
===============================================================================

BEYOND EXTERMINATION:
The Inquest enforces identity itself, not just hunting forbidden beings.

VERIFICATION DEMANDS (constant, without warning):
- Travel papers
- Residency permits
- Lineage records
- Magical certifications

FAILURE TO PRODUCE DOCUMENTATION:
- Treated as intent to deceive
- Detention often follows
- Disappearances "explained as administrative resolution"

SCRUTINY TARGETS (heaviest to lightest):
1. Beast folk (heaviest scrutiny)
2. Migrant laborers
3. Border populations
4. Anyone near vice districts
5. Mixed communities (viewed as "inherently unstable")

===============================================================================
COMPOSITION & CULTURE
===============================================================================

STRUCTURE:
- Small, mobile detachments
- Selected for loyalty and doctrinal compliance
- Trained to suppress personal hesitation

CULTURE:
- Emotional detachment encouraged
- Individual identity discouraged
- Uniforms immaculate
- Records meticulous
- Mercy not documented

BELIEFS:
- True believers: Many fully believe in their mandate
- Pragmatists: Others continue because "departure is not simple"
- Silence: Silence ensures survival

===============================================================================
RELATIONSHIPS WITH POWER

- Reality: Few wish to see them often

RACIAL RESPONSES:
✓ Elves: Comply through bureaucracy while limiting access
✓ Gnomes: Deny entry entirely
✓ Orcs: Avoid through mobility
✓ Dwarves: Restrict contact to fortified gates
✓ Beast Folk: Inspected relentlessly

HOLY EMPIRE:
