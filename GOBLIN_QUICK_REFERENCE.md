# Goblin Anti-Empire Quick Reference
**For Writers, Designers, and Content Creators**

---

## Core Goblin Identity

**Goblins are FIERCELY ANTI-EMPIRE.**

Core racial identity, on par with dwarven collectivism or orcish militarism. Non-negotiable.

---

## The Five Pillars

### 1. The Empire Is Illegitimate
- Built on stolen goblin land
- Massacred goblin populations
- Criminalizes goblin existence on ancestral territory
- Imperial "law" is theft with paperwork

### 2. No Recognition of Imperial Authority
- Don't recognize imperial courts
- Don't recognize imperial borders
- Don't recognize imperial "property rights" on stolen land
- Imperial law = occupier's law

### 3. Collaboration Is Species Betrayal
- Goblins who cooperate with empire are traitors
- Collaborators are shunned or eliminated
- No compromise, no reform, no "working within the system"
- Better to die resisting than live as slaves

### 4. Multi-Generational Liberation Struggle
- Not just survival—LIBERATION
- Every goblin knows stolen homeland names
- Every goblin knows imperial massacre methods
- Taught to children like breathing

### 5. The Empire Cannot Win
- Goblins have nothing left to lose
- Multi-generational commitment
- Home terrain advantage
- Decentralized structure (no capital to capture)
- Empire can only bleed

## What Goblins Do

### Resistance Activities
- Raid imperial supply lines
- Sabotage imperial infrastructure (roads, bridges, mines)
- Ambush imperial patrols
- Assassinate imperial officials (governors, commanders)
- Free imperial prisoners
- Steal imperial weapons to arm resistance
- Target collaborators for elimination
- Spread anti-imperial propaganda

### They Do NOT
- Negotiate with empire
- Recognize imperial courts
- Pay imperial taxes
- Apply for imperial permits
- Accept imperial "compensation" for stolen land
- Collaborate for survival
- Trust imperial promises

## How to Write Goblin Dialogue

### DO
✓ Express anti-empire ideology clearly
✓ Reference stolen lands and massacres
✓ Question legitimacy of imperial authority
✓ Show suspicion of humans (default assumption: imperial)
✓ Show solidarity with other anti-imperial groups
✓ Use oral tradition phrasing (teach/remember/preserve)
✓ Be willing to die for the cause

### DON'T
✗ Make goblins passive or defeated
✗ Have goblins seek "fair treatment" from empire
✗ Suggest goblins want "a place at the table"
✗ Frame resistance as greed or banditry
✗ Make goblins apologetic or submissive
✗ Suggest collaboration is acceptable
✗ Imply empire has legitimate authority

### Example Dialogue Pairs

**BAD:**
"Please don't report me to the guards. I'm just trying to survive."

**GOOD:**
"You imperial? Then get lost. You anti-imperial? Prove it."

---

**BAD:**
"We just want fair treatment from the empire."

**GOOD:**
"We don't want the empire's 'fair treatment.' We want our land back."

---

**BAD:**
"Maybe if we work with the empire, things will get better."

**GOOD:**
"Collaboration with the empire is extinction. There is no working with thieves."

## Goblin Reactions to Other Races

### Humans
**DEFAULT:** Suspicious (assume imperial until proven otherwise)
**IF ANTI-IMPERIAL:** Cautious allies
**IF IMPERIAL:** Hostile, targets

### Orcs
**Respect.** They understand fighting empires from weakness.
Potential allies against imperial targets.

### Lizard Folk (Shadow Fen)
**Allies.** Fellow victims of imperial aggression.
Intelligence sharing, safe houses, joint operations.

### Cat Folk (Beast Folk)
**Sympathetic.** Both displaced by empire.
Caravan networks help goblin couriers.

### Elves
**Frustrated.** Elves acknowledge goblin claims are valid but say "escalation is unproductive."
Goblins see this as complicity through inaction.

### Gnomes
**Frustrated.** Gnomes study goblin resistance as "asymmetric model."
Goblins see this as knowledge without action.

### Dwarves
**Neutral.** Dwarves seal tunnels to avoid goblin conflicts but won't fight FOR empire.
Mutual avoidance.

## Writing Goblin Books/Documents

### Appropriate Formats
- **Scrolls** (most common)
- **Scratched notes**
- **Songs** (oral tradition)
- **Memory lists**
- **Resistance manuals**

### Inappropriate Formats
- Formal tomes (implies formal education)
- Bound codices (too expensive/formal)
- Imperial-style official documents
- Academic treaties

### Tone
- Direct, uncompromising
- Memory-focused (lists, names, dates)
- Action-oriented (tactical advice)
- Ideologically clear
- Multi-generational perspective

### Example Titles
✓ "Why We Fight"
✓ "The Long Memory"
✓ "No One Is Illegal On Stolen Land"
✓ "On Collaboration"
✓ "The Eastern Cleansing: Survivor Accounts"

✗ "A Treatise on Goblin Philosophy"
✗ "The Academic Study of Resistance"
✗ "Towards a More Perfect Union with the Empire"

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Mistake 1: Making Goblins Passive
❌ "Goblins just want to survive quietly."
✓ "Goblins want LIBERATION, not quiet survival."

### Mistake 2: Both-Sidesing the Conflict
❌ "Both goblins and empire have valid points."
✓ "Present both perspectives with internal logic, but don't neutralize goblin grievances."

### Mistake 3: Suggesting Reform
❌ "Maybe goblins could work within the system for change."
✓ "Goblins reject the system as illegitimate. No reform of theft."

### Mistake 4: Forgetting Multi-Generational Nature
❌ "This goblin has given up the fight."
✓ "Individual goblins may die, but the resistance is cultural. Children are taught resistance from birth."

### Mistake 5: Making Collaboration Acceptable
❌ "Some goblins cooperate with the empire for survival."
✓ "Collaborators exist but are considered traitors. Shunned or eliminated."

## Testing Your Goblin Content

Ask these questions:

1. **Does this goblin express anti-empire ideology?**
   - If not, rewrite.

2. **Does this goblin recognize imperial authority?**
   - If yes, they're a collaborator (traitor) or it's wrong.

3. **Does this goblin want to work within the imperial system?**
   - If yes, rewrite. Goblins reject the system.

4. **Is this goblin apologetic or submissive?**
   - If yes, rewrite. Goblins are uncompromising.

5. **Does this content make empire seem legitimate?**
   - Empire can be powerful WITHOUT being legitimate.

6. **Does this trivialize imperial massacres?**
   - Goblins remember. Every. Single. One.

## Allied Faction Interactions

- Share intelligence on imperial movements
- Provide safe houses
- Joint operations against empire
- Mutual respect as fellow victims

### Orcs + Goblins
- Respect for guerrilla effectiveness
- Some joint raids
- Shared understanding of fighting empires
- "The enemy of my enemy"

- Provide false documentation
- Warn of imperial campaigns
- Ideological sympathy (both anti-imperial)

- Caravans carry goblin messages
- Both displaced by empire
- "Roads belong to travelers, not empires"
