                    CHANGES - January 25, 2026 (Session 6)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[NEW] Procedural Dungeon System (textrpg.lua)
  - Dungeons can now be entered from dungeon tiles on the world map
  - Press [E] when standing on a dungeon tile to enter
  - Each dungeon is procedurally generated with:
    * Random dungeon names (e.g., "Forsaken Catacombs", "Dark Depths")
    * 3-6 floors of increasing difficulty
    * Rooms connected by corridors and doors
    * Entrance and exit on each floor

[NEW] Dungeon Features
  - Stairs: Use > to descend, < to ascend between floors
  - Treasure Chests: Contains gold, potions, and rare items
    * Loot quality scales with floor depth
    * Common, Uncommon, and Rare loot tiers
  - Traps: Hidden dangers that deal damage when triggered
    * Damage scales with floor depth
  - NPCs: Prisoners and lost adventurers to rescue
    * Give rewards when rescued (gold, mana, items)
    * 5 different NPC types with unique dialogue

[NEW] Dungeon Enemies
  - Shallow floors (1-2): Dungeon Rats, Cave Spiders, Goblin Scouts
  - Mid floors (3-4): Skeleton Warriors, Orc Brutes, Shambling Corpses
  - Deep floors (5+): Cave Trolls, Wraiths, Dungeon Ogres
  - Bosses: Final floor guardians (Dungeon Drake, Lich Lord, Pit Fiend)
  - Enemy stats scale with player level

[NEW] Dungeon Exploration UI
  - Fog of war - only explored tiles are visible
  - Adjacent tiles are revealed as you move
  - Color-coded icons: E = Enemy, B = Boss, $ = Chest, ? = NPC
  - Current tile info and contextual actions displayed
  - Stats panel shows HP, MP, Gold, remaining enemies

[NEW] Dungeon Rewards
  - Clearing a dungeon grants bonus gold and XP
  - Bonus scales with total floors (50g and 100xp per floor)
  - Victory message and auto-exit when reaching the exit on final floor

[UPDATED] Camping in Dungeons
  - Camping in dungeons increases ambush chance by 50%
  - Guards are even more important in dungeon environments
  - Maximum ambush chance raised to 90% in dungeons

[UPDATED] Combat Return
  - Combat in dungeons returns to dungeon phase instead of world map
  - Inventory access from dungeon returns to dungeon

                    CHANGES - January 25, 2026 (Session 5)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[NEW] Party/Companion System (textrpg.lua)
  - Recruit companions at the Guild Hall to form a party
  - 6 Companion classes available:
    * Soldier: Sturdy frontline fighter (80 HP, 12 ATK, 10 DEF)
    * Archer: Ranged damage dealer (50 HP, 14 ATK, 5 DEF)
    * Battle Mage: Combat spellcaster (45 HP, 16 ATK, 4 DEF)
    * Healer: Keeps party alive, can heal allies (55 HP, 6 ATK, 6 DEF)
    * Thief: Quick striker with critical hits (40 HP, 13 ATK, 4 DEF)
    * Berserker: High damage, low defense (90 HP, 18 ATK, 2 DEF)
  - Maximum party size: 3 companions
  - Each companion has unique hire cost and daily wage
  - Companions scale with player level

[NEW] Guild Hall (Town menu)
  - Visit "Guild Hall" option in town to recruit companions
  - Shows 3-5 randomly generated companions for hire
  - Displays companion stats, class, portrait, hire cost, and daily wage
  - Can only hire if you have enough gold and party isn't full
  - Companions regenerate when revisiting guild

[NEW] Party Management (Town menu)
  - Visit "Manage Party" option to view current companions
  - Shows each companion's HP, stats, morale, and daily wage
  - Dismiss button to remove companions from party
  - Total daily wage cost displayed

[NEW] Companion Combat Integration
  - Companions join combat with their own initiative rolls
  - Initiative: d20 + level/3 for companions
  - Companions act automatically on their turn (AI-controlled)
  - Healers prioritize healing hurt allies (player or companions below 50% HP)
  - Party members displayed at bottom of combat screen with HP bars
  - Turn order panel shows companions with blue color coding
  - Turn indicator shows whose turn it is (player/companion/enemy)

[NEW] Enemy AI Targeting Update
  - Enemies can now target both player AND companions
  - 50% chance to target player, 50% chance to target random companion
  - Companions can be knocked out (shown as "DOWN" in combat)

[NEW] Companion Economy
  - Hire cost: Paid upfront when recruiting (varies by class and level)
  - Daily wages: Automatically deducted at start of each new day
  - If wages can't be paid:
    * Companions lose 15 morale per unpaid day
    * Companions with 0 morale leave the party automatically
  - Morale displayed in party management screen

[UPDATED] Rest at Inn
  - Resting now heals all party members to full HP as well

[UPDATED] Save/Load System
  - Party data persists between sessions
  - Existing saves automatically initialize empty party array
